Who we're here for

Real women. Real support.

The women we're here for are everywhere. The friend who never asks for help. The mom doing everything for everyone else. The woman rebuilding after loss, burnout, illness, or a life change she never saw coming.

Different stories, same feeling. Trying so hard to keep going while carrying way too much. If any of this sounds like you, we want to hear from you.

The caregiver

Pouring everything she has into the people she loves — kids, parents, partners, community — and running on empty herself. Working two jobs. Picking up extra shifts. Trying to get ahead of the bills.

The survivor

Navigating life after loss, illness, or something that broke her wide open. Still showing up every day. Still doing the work. But medical bills, funeral costs, and lost income have made getting back on her feet feel impossible.

The builder

Chasing something better — school, a new career, a business of her own. Putting in the hours even when no one's watching. Just needs a little breathing room to keep going.

Questions

Everything you want to know.

Who qualifies?
Applicants must be at least 18 years old, reside in the U.S., identify as a woman (including non-binary, trans, and all women-identified persons), and currently be navigating financial hardship. Qualifying does not guarantee selection, but every applicant is reviewed with care.
How long does the application take?
About two minutes. You'll provide your name, date of birth, and phone number, and confirm with an SMS code. Standard Giving then verifies your situation with a soft credit check. If selected, you'll be notified by email.
Will this affect my credit score?
No. A soft credit pull is used strictly to verify your financial situation and does not affect your credit score. It is not visible to lenders or other parties.
What financial information is visible?
Standard Giving sees a soft credit report: credit card balances, outstanding loans, delinquencies, and your overall financial snapshot. This is used only to confirm the hardship you describe, and is kept confidential.
How are recipients selected?
Standard Giving collects and verifies applicant information through the secure application process. Final grant decisions are made by Global Empowerment Mission Inc., the 501(c)(3) public charity that reviews each applicant and distributes all funds. Every application is read with care — qualifying does not guarantee selection, but we do our best to reach the women where support will make the biggest difference.
Who is behind this campaign?
The Daily Give is a community initiative by Katie Austin, in partnership with Standard Giving Co., which verifies applicants and operates the platform. Funds are distributed by Global Empowerment Mission Inc., a 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN: 45-3782061) with a 4-star Charity Navigator rating.
